Complete Oral Diagnostic Services

What We Offer

Our 4 Core Diagnostic Services

Every scan is performed with precision equipment and reported by certified dental radiologists — fast, accurate, affordable.

OPG – Orthopantomogram X-Ray

A single wide panoramic radiograph that captures all upper and lower teeth, both jawbones, the sinuses, and the TMJ joint in one image. Essential for extractions, orthodontics, and routine dental assessment.

  • Full mouth overview in one X-ray
  • No preparation required
  • Results ready same day
  • Ideal for dental check-ups, extractions, braces planning
Book OPG Scan

CBCT – Cone Beam 3D Imaging

Cone Beam Computed Tomography provides detailed 3D reconstruction of jaw, teeth, bone density, nerve canals, and soft tissue. The gold standard for complex dental diagnosis and surgical planning.

  • Full 3D view of jaw and teeth
  • Critical for implant placement & planning
  • Nerve canal identification
  • Available in full, half & focused field
Book CBCT Scan

Cephalometric X-Ray

A standardised lateral skull radiograph used extensively by orthodontists to analyse skeletal and dental relationships, jaw growth patterns, and plan braces or orthognathic surgery.

  • Full skull lateral view
  • Skeletal jaw relationship analysis
  • Essential for orthodontic treatment
  • Growth assessment for children
Book Ceph X-Ray

Intraoral X-Ray

High-resolution periapical and bitewing radiographs placed inside the mouth to capture detailed images of individual teeth, their roots, supporting bone, and interproximal surfaces.

  • Individual tooth root detail
  • Cavity and decay detection
  • Root canal assessment
  • Bone level evaluation
Book Intraoral X-Ray

Implant Planning

Comprehensive CBCT-based implant site assessment — measuring bone volume, density, and proximity to vital structures — to ensure safe and predictable implant placement outcomes.

  • Bone volume & density measurement
  • Nerve & sinus proximity mapping
  • Surgical stent planning support
  • Digital report for the implantologist
Book Implant Planning

TMJ Analysis

Detailed imaging of the temporomandibular joint to diagnose joint disorders, disc displacement, degenerative arthritis, condylar asymmetry, and bite-related pain or clicking.

  • Open & closed mouth CBCT views
  • Condylar position assessment
  • Articular disc evaluation
  • Jaw pain & clicking investigation
Book TMJ Analysis

Airway Analysis

3D volumetric assessment of the upper airway using CBCT imaging, used for diagnosing sleep apnea, airway obstruction, and planning orthodontic or surgical airway interventions.

  • Nasal & pharyngeal airway volume
  • Minimum cross-sectional area
  • Sleep apnea screening support
  • Orthodontic airway planning
Book Airway Analysis

Dental Photography

Clinical standardised dental photography for documentation, before-after treatment comparisons, insurance claims, patient communication, and professional case presentations.

  • Full face & smile photographs
  • Intraoral retracted views
  • Occlusal surface photos
  • High-resolution digital delivery
Book Photography
